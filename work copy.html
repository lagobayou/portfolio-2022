  • About Me

    All you have to do is mention something aquatic, then you'll have my attention!

    I was born in the Philippines and moved to Vancouver, British Columbia after spending nine years of my childhood in my homecountry. My passion for video games fuels a dream to influence or create the next big thing in the media industry.

    Prior to making up my mind, I was quite fond of animation believing animators were primarily responsible for the ideation of fictional universes seen in countless movies.

    It was only in 11th grade where I learned that other creative minds were involved ,thus I made my choice to pursue a career involving digital art and concept design.

    You'll often find me sipping on matcha lattes, bubble tea and iced tea. No fizzy drinks though, I quit drinking Coca Cola, Pepsi, Sprite in 2012 (though if it was a survival scenario, I would probably drink those!)
